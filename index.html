Hey friend, I'm Adriana, and I'm a graduate student at MIT! My research interests are numerical methods and algorithms, high-performance scientific computing, modeling and simulation, and computational geometry. I completed my B.S. in computer science with a minor in physics at the University of South Florida (May 2022), and am also a recipient of the NSF Graduate Research Fellowship (2022).

Post-graduate school, I eventually plan to pursue a career as professor or industry research scientist, with priority goals in (I) pushing for diversity and inclusivity in STEM, namely for women, people of color, and LGBTQ+ identities, and (II) helping to establish environmentally-conscious practices.

Outside of the lab (aka my computer), I help to lobby with Congress on environmental concerns as a Citizens' Climate Lobby volunteer, and enjoy playing piano, rock climbing, and creating meme art!
